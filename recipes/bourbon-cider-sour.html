Bourbon Cider Sour

Description

Add a little fall flavor into a basic whiskey sour. This bourbon apple cider takes smooth and smoky Jim Beam® Bourbon and mixes it with the crisp and tart tang of cold apple cider and orange juice. W

Ingredients

  • 2 parts Jim Beam® Bourbon
  • 3 parts cold Apple Cider/li>
  • 11 ⁄ 2  parts Orange Juice
  • 13 ⁄ 4  part Simple Syrup
  • Apple Slices

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Combine the bourbon, apple cider, orange juice, and simple syrup. Stir.

  2. Step 2

    Serve with an apple slice.
